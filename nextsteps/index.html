We appreciate that it can be difficult to plan out and take your next steps into higher education. The vast number of options available to you, along with the corresponding entry requirements and financial costs can be daunting and difficult to navigate through.

We are here to guide you through one of your most important life decisions.

Our team of Malaysian advisors are not only well informed with respect to the higher education application steps and processes, but they have also been through these application processes themselves. They have successfully gained admission to some of the world’s top universities and are well placed to give you the best advice for your next steps into higher education.

For a relatively small fee, you can gain access to one of our advisors that best suits your profile and requirements. Not only will they guide you through your application processes (via email, skype, facetime, whatsapp etc.), but you will also be able to ask them for general education or career-related advice post completion of your applications, with no pre-determined cut-off date! This could include the assessment of post-graduation options, as well as CV building advice.
