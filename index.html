About Us

Founded in July 2019, the Winacore Group is a multinational group headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

The group operates in several industries including, but not limited to: asset management (venture capital); software & IT consultancy; education; clothing; and food services.

The Winacore Group is committed to delivering high quality products and services, with a view of exceeding the needs of our customers.

We believe our philosophy will enable us to reach millions of customers in the years to come, and positively influence the markets and industries in which we are involved.

Team

Ashwin Chockalingam

Founder & Managing Director

The group's founder and managing director, Ashwin Chockalingam, has had stints at Ernst & Young (EY), Jones Lang LaSalle (JLL) and Pacific Investment Management Company (PIMCO) in London, with his experience covering private equity, investment banking, real estate, credit, structuring and tax.

He graduated from the London School of Economics with a First Class Honours in BS.c. Accounting & Finance.

He is also a Chartered Accountant.
