Our Process

An overview of our five-step due diligence and assessment process.

  • Step 1: Assessment

    Assessment of the business and venture from a commercial perspective.

  • Step 2: Due Diligence

    Assessment and verification of financial information, corporate structure and management teams.

  • Step 3: Proposed Offer

    A letter of offer with proposed terms will be issued post completion of due diligence.

  • Step 4: Documentation

    Upon acceptance of the offer, legal documentation will be drawn up to cover necessary detail with respect to the proposed terms.

  • Step 5: Completion

    Post signing of the legal documentation, funds will be released provided all conditions of the deal are satisfied.
